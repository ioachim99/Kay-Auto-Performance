Bumper to Bumper Warranty

Our "Bumper to Bumper" warranty covers all essential components of your fleet vehicles, providing you with peace of mind that all repair costs are managed efficiently and without surprises. This comprehensive warranty includes:

  • Engine and internal components: We cover any repair or replacement for engine components, including pistons, valves, crankshaft, and other critical parts.
  • Transmission and drivetrain system: The warranty covers repairs for the transmission, clutch, differential, and other components related to the drivetrain system.
  • Braking system: We ensure that the brakes are in optimal condition, covering all components, including calipers, discs, and drums.
  • Suspension system: We provide replacement and repair of shock absorbers, springs, and other suspension elements for optimal comfort.
  • Electrical system: The battery, alternator, starter, and other electrical components are fully covered.
  • Cooling system: The radiator, water pump, thermostat, and other cooling system components are included in the warranty.
  • Air conditioning and heating: We cover the climate control systems to ensure a comfortable environment for drivers.

With this warranty, you can have zero cost burdens for essential repairs, giving you complete predictability over your fleet's budget.
